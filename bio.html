Loralee Starla Gentry

I have quite the eclectic educational background. In 2008 I started out my collegiate career at California Polytechnic State University: San Luis Obispo studying Aerospace Engineering.

Although engineering was intriguing, my artistic side suffered greatly, so I went on to earn my Associate of Arts from Seattle Central Community College while I focused on finding my new educational path.

Finally I enrolled at Eastern Washington University and completed the junior level of the Film program. Now I am in my senior year at EWU studying Film, Design, and Art, headed for the field of digital media design and production upon graduation in the spring of 2015.
